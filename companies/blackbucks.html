BlackBuck is a leading logistics and transportation company in India, leveraging technology to streamline and optimize the supply chain. With a focus on digital freight matching, fleet management, and financial services, BlackBuck aims to transform the logistics industry. The company serves a wide range of clients, from small businesses to large enterprises, ensuring efficient and reliable transportation solutions.

Mission: To make logistics simple and efficient through technology and innovation.

Vision: To be the most trusted and preferred logistics partner, driving growth and success for businesses.
